The Artist

Meet Neena

“

I believe henna is a conversation between the skin and the soul. My work is about honoring the transition of your life's biggest moments with grace and beauty.

For over 25 years, I have been translating stories into skin through the ancient language of mehndi. What began as a childhood fascination in my grandmother's garden has evolved into a lifelong devotion.

I am committed to the purity of the craft, using only 100% natural, hand-mixed organic henna. No chemicals, no shortcuts—just the rich, earthy fragrance of tea, essential oils, and tradition.
