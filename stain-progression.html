What to Expect

The Stain Progression

Henna doesn't reveal its true color right away. Here's what happens between the moment the paste goes on and your final, richest stain.

water_drop Photo coming soon
Fresh Paste

0 Hours

The paste goes on a deep forest green and needs to stay put for 4–6 hours. This color is temporary — it's not the final stain.

nightlight Photo coming soon
After the Paste Comes Off

12 Hours

Once the dried paste flakes away, the skin underneath is a bright orange. It'll keep deepening in color over the next day.

favorite Photo coming soon
Full Oxidation

48 Hours

The stain reaches its darkest, richest reddish-brown — this is the color that will last through 1–3 weeks with proper aftercare.
