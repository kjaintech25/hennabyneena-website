What to Expect

The Stain Progression

Henna doesn't reveal its true color right away. Here's what happens between the moment the paste goes on and your final, richest stain.

Freshly Applied

Paste On

The paste goes on dark and needs to stay put for 4–6 hours. This is just the paste — not the final stain color underneath.

Right After Removal

0 Hours

Once the dried paste flakes away, the stain underneath starts out surprisingly light — a soft orange that will keep deepening over the next couple of days.

Still Deepening

12 Hours

The color keeps darkening and richening as it oxidizes, moving from orange toward a warm reddish-brown.

Full Oxidation

48 Hours

The stain reaches its darkest, richest reddish-brown — this is the color that will last through 1–3 weeks with proper aftercare.
